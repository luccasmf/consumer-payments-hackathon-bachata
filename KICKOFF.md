## Why

1. **Have fun and work with people you don't normally work with.**
2. **Lose the fear of agentic coding.** Engineers, designers, PMs, GMs, analysts — everyone ships code. AI does the heavy lifting; you steer.
3. **Feel what frontier models can do** managing real conversation, calling tools, holding state. The muscle our core chat needs.

## What we're building

A WhatsApp + AI conversational product. Three constraints:

- **Conversational** — dialogue is the primary interaction
- **WhatsApp-based** — runs on WhatsApp via Kapso
- **AI-driven** — the LLM is the brain, not a feature

Pick whatever idea your team wants inside those bounds.

## The one rule that matters

**Every team member pushes at least one commit to the team branch.**
Engineers, designers, PMs, GMs, analysts. No exceptions. AI tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot) are how non-engineers get there. A commit counts if it's a meaningful change you understood and authored — code, prompts, copy, eval cases, anything.
